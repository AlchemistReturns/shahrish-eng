Resident & Assistant Resident Engineers


Responsibilities:

  • Oversee all technical aspects of projects involving field survey and design, manage a variety of maintenance and construction projects in the field and provide specific instructions and information to other employees.
  • Deliver professional engineering services on various public sector engineering projects and serve as a liaison with the contractors, vendors and/or any other personnel on various complex engineering projects.
  • Respond to contractors by interpreting plans and specifications by the utilization of their engineering knowledge and experience.
  • Supervise, coordinate and track all and any field site activities and provide technical instruction on construction and/or design, execute remedial action using engineering design on projects to resolve issues.
  • Lead on-site junior engineers and inspectors to successfully observe work in progress and ensure that project records are properly maintained.
  • Monitor and maintain the progress of the project in relation with the proposal cost and schedule and hold any progress meetings as required.
  • Strictly implement and follow company and project safety protocols.

Qualifications:

  • Valid Professional Engineering License in the State of New York, with a bachelor’s degree in Civil Engineering or similar.
  • A minimum of 6 years of progressive experience working on public sector projects, preferably with NYCDDC/NYC DOT/NYS DOT/PANYNJ, or similar on projects involving highways, bridges, and street and structural work.
  • Ability to understand and interpret written specifications, plans, and construction documents for work orders and build documents.
  • Ability to effectively and tactfully communicate with clients, supervisors, and inspectors regarding specifications and qualification test results.
  • Detail-oriented, with capability to detect non-conformities during testing procedures and complete detailed reports within a timely manner.
  • A valid, clean driver’s license and personal vehicle to travel to job sites is preferred.

As we are expanding our services, client base and geographical boundaries, we are always looking for talented engineers, surveyors and construction professionals to meet our goal of engineering excellence. We offer a generous employee benefit package, and career growth opportunity. If you are self-motivated and looking for challenging and rewarding experience in your areas of expertise, we encourage you to send a resume to SSE.
