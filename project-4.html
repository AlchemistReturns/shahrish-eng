Structural Condition Assessment of Seven Existing Hospital Buildings

Project Description

SSE provided comprehensive structural condition assessments of seven (7) existing City of Orange Hospital Buildings. The following systems were tested and analyzed as part of the building assessment:
• Exterior - Inspection of Building Envelope: roof, exterior masonry, windows, doors and parapets
• Interior – Floor and wall, foundation, floor and roof construction, building and interior structural frame.
Upon completion of all inspection and analysis, SSE prepared a preliminary condition assessment report for the client.
