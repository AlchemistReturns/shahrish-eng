Structural Design for 8-Story Residential Building

Project Description

SSE provided structural, foundation and Geotechnical design for the proposed 8-story building. This building was planned for 38 units, 30,000 total square feet. The project consisted of a complete design of the structural steel framing, steel columns and matt slab foundations. This also included design of soldier pile shoring and underpinning.
