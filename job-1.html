NICET Inspectors


Candidates must possess at least one of the following AND a High School Diploma/equivalent:

  • Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent or Higher in Engineering.
  • Valid NICET Level II or Higher.
  • Relevant work experience is strongly preferred.
  • Basic understanding and ability to interpret written specifications, plans, and construction documents for work orders and build documents.
  • Ability to effectively communicate with clients, supervisors, and inspectors regarding specifications and qualification test results.
  • Detail-oriented, with capability to detect non-conformities during testing procedures and complete detailed reports within a timely manner.
  • Valid, clean driver’s license and personal vehicle to travel to and within project sites, as regular daily travel is required.

Qualified professionals will also have:

  • Ability to communicate with colleagues and clients of all levels.
  • Experience working with Microsoft Office Suite.
  • Ability to provide inspection reports in a timely manner.
  • Reliable means of transportation & valid driver’s license.
  • Willingness to obtain additional certifications.
  • Works well with a team and individually.
  • Familiarity with construction industry codes.

As we are expanding our services, client base and geographical boundaries, we are always looking for talented engineers, surveyors and construction professionals to meet our goal of engineering excellence. We offer a generous employee benefit package, and career growth opportunity. If you are self-motivated and looking for challenging and rewarding experience in your areas of expertise, we encourage you to send a resume to SSE.
